NEW SOUTH WALES

New South Wales lies in the eastern coast of Australia bordering with Queensland to the north, Victoria to the south, South Australia to the west, and Tasmanian sea to the east. The total area of New South Wales is 809,444 sq. km and its total population is 8,071,071. The capital city of New South Wales is Sydney.
