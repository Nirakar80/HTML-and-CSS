WESTERN AUSTRALIA

Western Australia is the largest state of Australia. It covers one third of the total land of the Australia and lies on the westmost part of the country. It is bounded by Indian ocean on its north and west, Northern Territory to its north-east and South Australia to its south-east, and Southern Ocean to its South. Its total area is 2,645,615 sq. km and the total population is 2,606,338. The capital city of Western Australia is Perth.
