NORTHERN TERRITORY

Northern Territory abbreviated as NT is a central Australian state to the northern part of the country. NT is the third largest federal division of Autsralia. It is surrounded by Western Australia on the west, Queensland on the east, South Australia on the south and Timor sea on the north. Its total area is 1,420,970 sq. km and the total population is 245,854. The capital city of the state is Darwin.
