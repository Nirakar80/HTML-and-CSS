QUEENSLAND

Queensland is the second largest state of Australia. Its neighbouring states are Northern Territory on the west, South Australia on the south west, and New South Wales on the south. On the east of Queensland lies Pacific Ocean and Coral sea. The area of Queensland is 1,852,642 sq. km and the total population is 5,076,512. The capital city of Queensland is Brisbane.
