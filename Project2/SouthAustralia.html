SOUTH AUSTRALIA

South Australia is a south-central state of Australia with a total area of 1,043,514 sq. km and total population 1,748,630. It has Western Australia on its west, Queensland on its north-east, New South Wales on its east, Victoria on its south-east and Great Australian Bight on its south. South Australia borders with the highest number of states of Australia. The capital city of South Australia is Adelaide.
