VICTORIA

Victoria is the south-eastern state of Australia. It is the most densely populated state among any other states of Australia. It shares its boundary with New South Wales on its north, South Australia on the west, and Tasmanean sea on the south. Victoria has a area of 237,659 sq. km and comprises population of 6,566,170. The capital city of Victoria is Melbourne.
